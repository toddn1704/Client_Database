BASIC INSTRUCTIONS: 

1. Select your name from the list.
2. Press the 'Login' button to proceed to the database.
3. Press the 'View' button to see and make changes to your information.

 
ADDING NEW VOLUNTEERS:

1. Go to the "Actions" menu and select the "Add New Volunteer" option.
2. Enter the new volunteer's information.
3. Press 'Save' to add the new volunteer.

 
VIEWING ACTIVE MEMBERS:

1. Go to the "Actions" menu and select the "View Active Volunteers" option.
2. You will see a list of all volunteers who are currently on the food pantry rotation.
3. Select a member and press the 'View' button to see the volunteer's information.

 
VIEWING ALL MEMBERS:

1. Go to the "Actions" menu and select the "View All Volunteers" option.
2. You will see a list of all volunteers, whether they are active or not.
3. Select a member and press the 'View' button to see the volunteer's information.
